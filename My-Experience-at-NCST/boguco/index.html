Overview

Nasser Centre for Science and Technology (NCST)

The Nasser Centre for Science and Technology (NCST), established in 2014 in line with the vision of His Majesty King Hamad bin Isa Al Khalifa, aims to shape Bahrain's future through its youth. Chaired by His Highness Shaikh Nasser bin Hamad Al Khalifa, the centre focuses on equipping youth with the knowledge and skills needed for advanced sectors like technology, energy, and engineering. It offers innovative educational programs in fields such as Artificial Intelligence, Cybersecurity, and Cloud Computing, and features a Cybersecurity Academy and corporate training initiatives. NCST strives to be a regional hub for technological solutions through collaborations with local and global institutions.
